## Core outreach angles

1. International SEO failures are usually systems failures, not just content failures.
2. Hreflang is not the first problem. Market logic and content parity are.
3. US companies entering new markets need authority inputs, not just translated pages.
4. Same-language markets still require localization and proof adaptation.
5. International SEO should be measured by market-level pipeline, not global traffic totals.
