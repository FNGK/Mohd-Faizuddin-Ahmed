Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information SEO With Faiz collects when you use seowithfaiz.com, submit an inquiry, or respond to one of our LinkedIn lead forms — how we use it, who we share it with, and the rights you have over it.

Last updated: 12 July 2026

Who we are

SEO With Faiz ("we", "us", "our") is a web design, development, SEO, and performance-marketing studio operated by Mohd Faizuddin Ahmed. For any privacy question, or to exercise a right described below, contact win@seowithfaiz.com. We act as the data controller for the personal information described here.

Information we collect

Information you give us directly

  • Website inquiry form: your name, work email, website URL, primary market, and the details you write about your goals.
  • LinkedIn lead forms: if you submit one of our lead-generation forms on LinkedIn, we receive the information you choose to share through it — typically your name, professional email, company, and job title — passed to us by LinkedIn.
  • Direct contact: anything you send us by email or messaging apps (for example, WhatsApp) when you reach out.

Information collected automatically

  • Analytics: we use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the site (pages viewed, approximate location, device and browser type, and referring source). This data is pseudonymous and not used to identify you personally.
  • Advertising measurement: when we run LinkedIn advertising, the LinkedIn Insight Tag may record that a visit or conversion originated from a LinkedIn ad, so we can measure results and reach relevant audiences. You can control this through your LinkedIn ad settings.
  • Security and delivery logs: our hosting and CDN provider (Cloudflare) processes technical data such as IP address and request metadata to serve the site securely and prevent abuse.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiry and discuss whether we're a fit for your project.
  • To provide, scope, and deliver the services you engage us for.
  • To measure and improve our website and marketing, including advertising performance.
  • To keep the site secure and to comply with our legal obligations.

Where the law requires a legal basis (for example, under the UK/EU GDPR), we rely on your consent (analytics and advertising cookies), our legitimate interest in running and growing the studio, and the performance of a contract when we work together.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate, and only for the purposes above:

  • LinkedIn (advertising and lead forms)
  • Google (Analytics)
  • Cloudflare (hosting, CDN, and security)
  • Our email provider (to receive and reply to inquiries)

These providers process data under their own terms and only as needed to provide their service to us. We may also disclose information if required by law.

International transfers

We serve clients in the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, Europe, and elsewhere, and we operate from India. Your information may therefore be processed outside your country. Where required, our providers use recognised safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) for these transfers.

How long we keep it

We keep inquiry and client information for as long as needed to respond to you and deliver our services, and thereafter only as required for legitimate business or legal reasons. Analytics data is retained according to our Google Analytics settings. You can ask us to delete your information at any time.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your personal information, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to withdraw consent. If you are in the United Kingdom or European Union, these rights come from the GDPR. If you are a California resident, the CCPA/CPRA gives you rights to know, delete, and correct your information and to opt out of "sale" or "sharing" — we do not sell your personal information.

To exercise any right, email win@seowithfaiz.com. We will respond within the timeframe the applicable law requires. You also have the right to complain to your local data-protection authority.

Cookies and tracking

The site uses a small number of cookies and similar technologies for analytics and, when advertising is active, for measuring ad performance. When these are in use, you'll see a consent banner on your first visit and can accept, reject, or choose which categories to allow. You can change your choice at any time: manage your cookie choices. You can also block or delete cookies in your browser settings, opt out of Google Analytics using Google's opt-out browser add-on, and manage LinkedIn ad tracking in your LinkedIn account's ad-preferences settings.

Children

Our services are for businesses and are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our services or legal obligations change. When we do, we'll revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will be made clear on this page.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email win@seowithfaiz.com and you'll hear back from Faiz directly.
