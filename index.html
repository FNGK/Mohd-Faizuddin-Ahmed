Answers

Direct answers before you book a call.

What do you actually deliver?

Everything from the first wireframe to next quarter's growth: web design and development (custom, 3D, WordPress, Shopify and Shopify Plus, Wix, and Magento), technical and international SEO, and performance marketing. One person owns all of it, so nothing falls through the cracks between vendors.

Who is this built for?

Enterprise and growth-stage brands that treat their website as revenue infrastructure, not a brochure. If you want a premium, senior-led partner with hands on the actual work, we'll get on well. If you need the cheapest option, I'm honestly not it.

Do you guarantee rankings or results?

No, and I'd be cautious of anyone who does. Neither Google nor the AI answer engines sell guaranteed positions. What I commit to is senior execution, technically excellent work, and honest reporting tied to pipeline, so you always see what's moving and why.

How do we start, and what does it cost?

It starts with a strategy call where I review your site and goals and lay out the highest-impact path. There's no rate card — every engagement is scoped individually and you get a tailored proposal, whether that's a project build or an ongoing growth partnership. If a smaller fix would solve your problem, I'll tell you.
