SEO Audit Case Study

Unstop SEO audit framework

This case study documents a 10-part SEO audit built to turn broad diagnostics into a clear execution sequence.

Context

The objective was to review organic performance blockers and define high-impact SEO priorities in a practical format.

Audit scope

  • Technical SEO health and crawl/index signals.
  • On-page quality and content structure.
  • Internal linking, schema opportunities, and Core Web Vitals.
  • Competitor patterns and execution priority mapping.

Approach

  • Break findings into focused thematic modules.
  • Rank recommendations by impact and ease of implementation.
  • Translate findings into a 30-day action sequence.

Outcome

The audit produced a practical execution roadmap, reducing ambiguity and helping stakeholders start with the highest-value fixes.

Tools used

Search Console, Lighthouse, structured SEO analysis framework, schema review methods.
