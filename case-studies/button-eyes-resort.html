The work

Hospitality sites live or die on trust and friction.

A resort website is a conversion machine with a very short attention window: photos, rooms, price confidence, and a booking path with no surprises. The engagement covered the platform, the funnel, and the search visibility feeding it.

01 · Platform

WordPress, kept lean

Site structure and page templates built for speed and editorial control — rooms, experiences, and offers the team can manage without a developer on call.

02 · Funnel

Booking-first UX

Clear room presentation, honest policy communication, and a path to the booking engine designed to lose fewer travelers on the way.

03 · Search

Technical SEO recovery

A full diagnostic across indexation, duplicate URLs, metadata, and local intent — documented in an audit pack the client (and you) can read end to end.
