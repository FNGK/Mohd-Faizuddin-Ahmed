The brief

One partner from first wireframe to live site.

The academy needed more than a brochure. Enrollment is seasonal and competitive; parents research seriously before trusting an academy with their kids. The site had to communicate professional standards — the same standards the academy applies on the pitch — and be found when families search.

01 · Structure

Architecture around real questions

Programs by age phase (foundation, development, elite), coaching approach, achievements, matches, and enrollment — organized the way parents actually evaluate an academy, not the way an org chart looks.

02 · Build

Custom Next.js, no template

A hand-built Next.js front end: fast image handling for match photography, dynamic routing for news and matches, and a component system the site can grow on.

03 · Search

Found by the right families

Clean semantic structure, local-intent pages, and metadata built for how Hyderabad parents search — so discovery doesn't depend on paid boosts alone.

Why this case matters to you

The same discipline scales to any brand.

Strip away the football and this is the pattern every engagement follows: understand how the audience decides, structure the site around those decisions, build it fast and custom, and make it findable. That pattern doesn't care what industry you're in.
