GSC Error Priority Calculator

Use this calculator to decide which technical SEO errors to fix first based on business impact and effort. It is designed for teams that need practical sprint prioritization.

Scoring model

Priority Score = (Impact × 0.45) + (Page Value × 0.35) + (Issue Volume × 0.20) − (Effort × 0.30)

Higher scores indicate higher priority for immediate resolution.
