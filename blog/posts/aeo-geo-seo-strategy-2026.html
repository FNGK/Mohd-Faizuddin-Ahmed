AEO, GEO, and SEO strategy

How to build an AEO and GEO-ready SEO strategy in 2026

Quick answer: combine technical SEO hygiene, intent-driven content design, and trust signals that support both search engines and AI answer systems.

Start with technical integrity before channel expansion

AI visibility does not replace core SEO hygiene. You still need crawlability, index clarity, clean internal links, and strong page architecture. If technical quality is weak, content cannot perform consistently in either traditional search or answer-driven experiences.

Baseline checklist for growth teams

  • Resolve index and crawl conflicts on high-value pages first.
  • Align page intent and heading structure with one primary search need.
  • Add structured data only where it improves semantic clarity.

Build entities and trust signals, not just keyword pages

Generative systems rely on entity confidence and reference quality. That means your brand, people, services, and evidence should be clearly represented across your website and supporting mentions.

Use consistent author signals, case studies, citations, and service definitions. This improves both answer relevance and business credibility.

Map content by decision stage, not just topic volume

Content should support real buyer progression. Top-of-funnel assets educate, mid-funnel assets validate, and bottom-funnel assets reduce decision friction. This is where AEO and conversion goals connect.

  • Top-funnel: educational explainers and trend analysis.
  • Mid-funnel: frameworks, comparisons, and case evidence.
  • Bottom-funnel: service pages with direct scope and CTA clarity.
