# LinkedIn Premium Playbook — manual, run by Faiz (the team prepares)

> LinkedIn is the #1 room where $1K–$5K buyers sit. Organic POSTING is
> automated via Buffer. Everything below is MANUAL — LinkedIn forbids
> automation and the account was recently restricted, so never use bots or
> browser automation here. The Strategist refreshes the searches/templates
> weekly; Faiz spends ~15 focused minutes/day executing.

## 0. One-time setup (30 min, do once)
- Apply the optimized Headline, About, Services, Featured, banner (already drafted).
- Turn ON **Creator Mode** + "Providing Services". Custom URL `/in/seowithfaiz`.
- Confirm Premium tier: **Sales Navigator Core** is the target tool (best ROI
  for this). Premium Business also works but Sales Nav's lead filters + saved
  searches + InMail volume are the edge.

## 1. Sales Navigator — saved lead searches (the ICP, pre-built)

Create these as **saved searches** (Sales Nav → Lead filters). Save each; Sales
Nav alerts you to new matches weekly — that's your daily prospect feed.

**A. Boutique hospitality decision-makers**
- Job titles: Owner, General Manager, Founder, Director of Marketing, Revenue Manager
- Industry: Hospitality, Leisure/Travel/Tourism
- Company headcount: 11–200
- Geography: United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, Ireland
- Keywords: "boutique hotel" OR "inn" OR "bed and breakfast" OR "resort"

**B. Sports clubs & academies**
- Job titles: Owner, Director, Academy Director, Founder, Head of Operations, Marketing Manager
- Industry: Sports, Spectator Sports, Health/Wellness/Fitness
- Company headcount: 2–200
- Geography: US, UK, Canada, Australia
- Keywords: "academy" OR "soccer" OR "football club" OR "swim school" OR "sports club"

**C. Warm-intent triggers (highest priority — act within 48h)**
- Sales Nav filter: "Posted on LinkedIn in past 30 days" + "Changed jobs in
  past 90 days" on searches A/B. New role or active posting = open to change.
- Also work **"Who viewed your profile"** (Premium) daily — anyone from A/B
  who viewed you is a warm connect.

## 2. The daily 15-minute cadence
1. **Engage first, pitch never (5 min):** on 3–5 prospects from A/B, leave one
   genuinely useful comment on a recent post (not "great post!" — add an
   insight). This warms them before any outreach.
2. **Connect with a note (5 min):** send 3–5 connection requests with a
   specific, non-salesy note (template below). No pitch in the connect.
3. **Follow up / InMail (5 min):** for accepted connects or high-fit prospects
   you can't connect with, send the teardown InMail (template below).

## 3. Message templates (personalize the [brackets] every time)

**Connection note (no pitch):**
> Hi [Name] — came across [Business] while looking at how [boutique inns /
> academies] handle their booking pages. Genuinely nice [specific detail].
> Would love to connect.

**InMail — the teardown offer (this is the closer):**
> Hi [Name], I build and grow websites for [boutique hotels / sports
> academies], and I took a real look at [business.com]. One quick thing:
> [ONE specific, verified issue — e.g. "your book-now button sits below the
> fold on mobile, so a chunk of ready-to-book visitors never see it"].
> That's usually a quiet revenue leak. Happy to send a free 10-minute
> teardown video of your site — no pitch, just the fixes I'd make first.
> Want me to record it? — Faiz, seowithfaiz.com

## 4. Content on LinkedIn (semi-automated)
- Buffer posts 3×/week (the Social Manager drafts; audit-gated). Faiz should
  ADD one native, unscheduled comment-reply pass daily — replying to
  commenters within the first hour is what the algorithm rewards.
- Repost each new blog/case study as a LinkedIn "text + link in first comment"
  (link-in-comment beats link-in-post for reach).

## 5. What "max value" from Premium actually looks like
- Sales Nav saved-search alerts = a fresh, filtered prospect list weekly (no
  guessing).
- InMail = reach decision-makers you're not connected to (the teardown offer).
- "Who viewed you" + profile-view spikes after posts = warm inbound to work.
- Premium learning/insights = credibility signals on the profile.
