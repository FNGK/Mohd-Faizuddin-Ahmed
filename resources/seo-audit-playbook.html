SEO audit playbook for growth teams

Quick answer: start with technical blockers on revenue pages, then align intent and internal links before expanding content volume.

This playbook is built for teams that need execution-ready SEO priorities. It avoids long theory and focuses on decisions that affect business outcomes.

Step 1: Confirm technical integrity

  • Validate crawl/index behavior for priority pages.
  • Review Core Web Vitals and major rendering blockers.
  • Check canonical and metadata consistency.

Step 2: Align pages to intent

  • Map one primary intent per key page.
  • Fix weak heading structure and thin sections.
  • Prioritize conversion-linked pages first.

Step 3: Build a 30-day action stack

  • Week 1: technical blockers
  • Week 2: on-page and internal link improvements
  • Week 3: content gap actions
  • Week 4: measurement and iteration
