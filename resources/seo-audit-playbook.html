SEO audit playbook for small businesses

This playbook helps you run a practical SEO audit. It is built for small businesses that need clear actions, not long theory.

Who this is for

  • Business owners with low organic traffic.
  • Lean teams that need SEO priorities for the next 30 days.
  • Founders who want to fix search basics before scaling content or ads.

Step 1: Check technical SEO first

  1. Confirm crawlability and indexability of key pages.
  2. Review Core Web Vitals and page speed issues.
  3. Check metadata, canonicals, and internal linking health.

Step 2: Review on-page SEO for priority pages

  1. Pick your top 10 pages by business value.
  2. Map each page to one clear search intent.
  3. Fix title tags, headings, and thin content sections.

Step 3: Build a 30-day action plan

Week 1: Technical fixes and indexing issues.

Week 2: On-page updates for core pages.

Week 3: Internal linking and content refresh.

Week 4: Re-check rankings, clicks, and conversions.

What good looks like

  • Fewer technical errors on high-value pages.
  • Better alignment between page intent and keyword targeting.
  • A clear next-step list your team can execute without confusion.
