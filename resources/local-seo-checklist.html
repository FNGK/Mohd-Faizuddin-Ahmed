Local SEO checklist for one-location businesses

This checklist helps you improve local SEO in 30 days. It focuses on practical fixes that improve local search visibility.

Week 1: Google Business Profile basics

  • Set the right primary category.
  • Complete key profile fields and business details.
  • Match business name, address, and phone with website details.

Week 2: NAP consistency and local trust

  • Review NAP consistency on key directories and social profiles.
  • Fix mismatched business details.
  • Use one standard format across all listings.

Week 3: Local landing page quality

  • Add clear local service intent to title and headings.
  • Include location signals naturally in core sections.
  • Improve internal links to the local page from relevant pages.

Week 4: Measure and refine

  • Check search visibility and click trends.
  • Track calls, leads, and form submissions.
  • Refine pages with low engagement or weak local intent.
