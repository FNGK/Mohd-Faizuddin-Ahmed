30-day local SEO checklist for service businesses

Quick answer: improve profile quality, clean citation consistency, and strengthen local landing page intent before scaling content output.

Use this checklist when local leads matter more than broad traffic. It focuses on practical actions for map visibility, location pages, and conversion quality.

Week 1: GBP and local baseline

  • Validate primary category and service descriptors.
  • Check business details for consistency.
  • Review profile quality and conversion readiness.

Week 2: Citation consistency

  • Audit core citations and social business listings.
  • Fix inconsistent name, address, and phone data.
  • Document citation governance for future updates.

Week 3: Local landing page quality

  • Improve page intent and heading clarity.
  • Add trust signals and practical conversion CTAs.
  • Strengthen internal links to local service pages.

Week 4: Measurement and next actions

  • Track visibility and conversion-oriented engagement signals.
  • Identify page-level weaknesses and iterate quickly.
  • Plan monthly local SEO operating rhythm.
