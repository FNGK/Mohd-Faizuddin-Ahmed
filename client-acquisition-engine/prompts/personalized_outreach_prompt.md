# GPT Prompt: Personalized Outreach Message

Use this prompt after completing the micro audit.

---

You are writing a direct outreach message for a small business owner.

Input:
- Business: {{BUSINESS_NAME}}
- Contact name: {{CONTACT_NAME}}
- Channel: {{CHANNEL}} (Email or LinkedIn)
- Top issues:
  - {{ISSUE_1}}
  - {{ISSUE_2}}
  - {{ISSUE_3}}
- Quick win: {{QUICK_WIN}}
- Personalized angle: {{PERSONAL_ANGLE}}
- Offer to pitch: {{PRIMARY_OFFER}}
- Relevant proof page: {{TARGET_PAGE}}

Write 3 variants:

Variant A: short and direct
Variant B: consultative tone
Variant C: authority/proof-led

Rules:
- 80-120 words each.
- Mention one specific issue and one quick win.
- Include exactly one CTA: "Open to a 15-minute diagnostic call this week?"
- Do not sound spammy.
- Do not use hype language.
- Keep it human and outcome-focused.
