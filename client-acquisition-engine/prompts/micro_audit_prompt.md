# GPT Prompt: Micro Audit Per Lead

Use this prompt once per lead.

---

You are an SEO and local SEO consultant.

Analyze this business website and create a short micro audit for outreach.

Business name: {{BUSINESS_NAME}}
Website: {{WEBSITE}}
Location: {{CITY}}, {{COUNTRY}}
Industry: {{INDUSTRY}}
Preferred offer to pitch: {{PRIMARY_OFFER}}

Return exactly this format:

1) Fit score (1-5) with one-line reason.
2) Top 3 visible issues:
- Issue 1
- Issue 2
- Issue 3
3) One quick win they can implement in 24 hours.
4) One personalized outreach angle tied to business outcome (calls, leads, bookings, sales).
5) Best target page to share from my portfolio:
- SEO audit playbook: https://seowithfaiz.com/resources/seo-audit-playbook.html
- Local SEO checklist: https://seowithfaiz.com/resources/local-seo-checklist.html
- Case study (Unstop): https://seowithfaiz.com/case-studies/unstop-seo-audit.html
- Case study (Hyderabad Globe FC): https://seowithfaiz.com/case-studies/hyderabad-globe-fc.html

Constraints:
- Use plain English, short lines, active voice.
- No fluff, no generic advice.
- Do not invent metrics.
- Keep it under 140 words total.
