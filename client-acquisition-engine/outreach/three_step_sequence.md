# Three-Step Outreach Sequence

This sequence is built for personalized outreach only.

## Step 1: First Touch (Day 1)

Subject ideas:
- Quick SEO idea for {{BUSINESS_NAME}}
- Noticed one local SEO gap on {{BUSINESS_NAME}}
- One practical growth fix for your site

Message:

Hi {{CONTACT_NAME}},

I reviewed {{BUSINESS_NAME}} and noticed one issue that may be costing qualified traffic: {{ISSUE_1}}.

A quick fix you can apply fast is {{QUICK_WIN}}.

I do focused {{PRIMARY_OFFER}} work for businesses, and I documented a practical framework here: {{TARGET_PAGE}}.

Open to a 15-minute diagnostic call this week?

Regards,  
Faiz

## Step 2: Follow-Up (Day 4)

Subject: Re: quick SEO idea for {{BUSINESS_NAME}}

Hi {{CONTACT_NAME}},

Quick follow-up in case this got buried.

The fastest win I see is still {{QUICK_WIN}}. If useful, I can share a short priority list you can execute this month.

Open to a 15-minute diagnostic call this week?

Regards,  
Faiz

## Step 3: Final Bump (Day 8)

Subject: Last note on {{BUSINESS_NAME}} SEO

Hi {{CONTACT_NAME}},

Last note from my side.

If SEO is not a focus right now, no problem. If it is, I can send a one-page action snapshot based on {{ISSUE_1}} and {{ISSUE_2}}.

Open to a 15-minute diagnostic call this week?

Regards,  
Faiz

## Execution Rules

- Never send generic bulk emails.
- Always personalize at least one issue and one quick win.
- Stop after step 3 if no response.
- Log every touch in CRM with next follow-up date.
