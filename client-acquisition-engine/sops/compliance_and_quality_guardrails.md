# Compliance and Quality Guardrails

Use these rules to protect deliverability, brand trust, and long-term growth.

## Outreach Compliance

- Send personalized outreach only.
- Do not scrape or blast mass lists.
- Stop sequence after 3 touches if no response.
- Always identify yourself clearly.
- Do not make false claims or fabricated metrics.

## Deliverability Basics

- Keep daily sends low while warming manually.
- Use plain-text style emails.
- Avoid spam trigger words and excessive links.
- Keep one clear CTA only.

## Lead Qualification Rules

- Prioritize fit score 4-5 first.
- Skip low-fit prospects unless pipeline is empty.
- Focus one niche at a time until first close.

## Sales Integrity

- Sell scope you can deliver in stated timeline.
- Confirm exactly what is included and excluded.
- Do not promise ranking guarantees.

## Asset-Building Rule

- Every closed project must produce:
  - one testimonial request,
  - one reusable proof snippet,
  - one referral ask after early wins.
