# Delivery SOP (Starter Offers + Retainer Upsell)

## A) SEO Audit SOP

### Inputs required
- Website URL
- Top 3 business goals
- Top 3 pages to prioritize
- Access to analytics tools if available

### Delivery checklist
- Technical and on-page review for up to 10 pages.
- Prioritized issue list (high, medium, low impact).
- 30-day execution roadmap.
- One 30-minute Q&A call.

### Deliverables
- 1 PDF/Doc action plan.
- 1 follow-up summary message.

## B) Local SEO Sprint SOP

### Inputs required
- Google Business Profile link
- Service area and priority locations
- Existing local pages

### Delivery checklist
- GBP basics and category review.
- NAP consistency checks.
- Local page structure and metadata fixes list.
- 30-day local visibility action plan.

### Deliverables
- 1 local SEO checklist report.
- 1 follow-up summary message.

## C) Retainer Upsell SOP

Upsell trigger:
- Client asks for implementation help after audit.

Retainer inclusions (starter):
- Monthly implementation of approved priorities.
- Basic reporting dashboard updates.
- One monthly strategy call.

Retainer close line:
- "Now that priorities are clear, I can implement and track this monthly so results compound."

## D) Quality Control

- Every recommendation must map to business outcome.
- Keep language short, direct, and actionable.
- No generic audit templates without client context.
- End every report with "Do this first this week."

## E) Capacity Guardrail

- Maximum active starter projects at once: 5.
- If above limit, move new client start dates forward.
- Never accept work you cannot deliver on time.
