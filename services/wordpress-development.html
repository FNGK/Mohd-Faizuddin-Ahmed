Straight talk

Is this the right call for you?

I would rather point you at the right tool than sell you the wrong one. Here is when this fits, and when it does not.

Great fit when…

  • Your team publishes and edits content regularly
  • You want a mature plugin and integration ecosystem
  • You need a flexible marketing site or blog at its core
  • You are rescuing a slow or hacked WordPress site

Probably not, if…

  • You are running high-volume commerce (Shopify Plus or Magento usually wins)
  • You need bespoke app-like behavior (a custom or headless build fits better)
  • You want the absolute simplest self-managed site (Wix Studio may be enough)

Questions

Common questions

Is WordPress good for SEO?

Yes, WordPress is excellent for SEO when it is built well. The problems people blame on WordPress are usually bloated themes, too many plugins, and slow hosting. I strip that back so the CMS helps your rankings instead of fighting them.

Will my WordPress site actually be fast?

It will if it is built for speed rather than assembled from a heavy page builder. I target a 95-plus Lighthouse score with proper caching, optimized images, and a lean theme, and I will show you the numbers.

Can you rescue an existing WordPress site?

Often, yes. Slow, hacked, or inherited-mess WordPress sites are a common request. I audit what is there, fix the performance and security issues, and only rebuild the parts that genuinely need it.
