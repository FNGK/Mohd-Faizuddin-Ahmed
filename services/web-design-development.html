Questions

What clients usually ask.

How long does a website build take?

A focused marketing site is usually four to eight weeks; a larger store or custom build runs longer. I'd rather give you a real timeline after seeing your scope than a number that falls apart in week two.

Will the new site be good for SEO?

That's the whole point of building it myself. Clean semantic markup, fast Core Web Vitals, sensible URL and internal-link structure, and schema all go in during the build, not as an afterthought — so you don't launch and then discover you have to rebuild for search.

Can you redesign or rebuild our existing site?

Yes. Redesigns and platform migrations are common. The key is protecting the rankings and traffic you already have, so I plan redirects, preserve URL equity, and migrate content carefully rather than starting from zero.
