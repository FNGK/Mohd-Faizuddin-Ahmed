Magento and Adobe Commerce for complex, high-volume catalogs.

When you have thousands of SKUs, complex pricing, or serious B2B requirements, the friendly all-in-one platforms start to strain. Magento (Adobe Commerce) is built for exactly that scale, and it needs someone who will keep it fast rather than let it sprawl.

Is this the right call for you?

I would rather point you at the right tool than sell you the wrong one. Here is when this fits, and when it does not.

Great fit when…

  • You have thousands of SKUs or complex product data
  • You need B2B, custom pricing, or multi-store setups
  • You require deep ERP or systems integration
  • Off-the-shelf platforms cannot model your business

Probably not, if…

  • You are a straightforward DTC store (Shopify Plus is usually simpler)
  • You have a small team with no dev support
  • You want the lowest possible maintenance overhead

Questions

Common questions

Magento or Shopify Plus?

If your catalog and business rules are complex, with big SKU counts, intricate B2B pricing, and multi-store, Magento's flexibility earns its keep. If you are a more straightforward DTC brand, Shopify Plus is usually faster to run and cheaper to maintain. I will give you an honest read for your situation.

Is Magento hard to maintain?

It is more demanding than hosted platforms, yes, which is exactly why it needs disciplined engineering. Done right, with performance and upgrades handled properly, it is a powerhouse; neglected, it slows to a crawl.

Can you speed up our existing Magento store?

Usually. Slow Magento is one of the most common complaints, and much of it comes down to caching, indexing, image handling, and front-end weight, all fixable without a full rebuild.
