Wix Studio Development

Wix Studio sites that look custom, without a dev on call.

Wix has a reputation problem it mostly does not deserve anymore. Wix Studio, built properly, is a genuinely capable platform for lean teams that want a polished site they can manage themselves. The trick is building it like a designer, not clicking a template together.

In short: I design and build Wix Studio sites for small and lean teams — custom-looking layouts, CMS collections, and proper SEO setup — so you get a professional site you can update yourself without hiring a developer.

Straight talk

Is this the right call for you?

I would rather point you at the right tool than sell you the wrong one. Here is when this fits, and when it does not.

Great fit when…

  • You are a lean team that wants to self-manage the site
  • You want a fast, efficient launch with a polished result
  • You value ease of editing over deep custom functionality
  • You do not want to maintain hosting or plugins

Probably not, if…

  • You are competing in a brutally SEO-competitive niche (custom or WordPress may edge it)
  • You need complex commerce or custom app logic
  • You expect to need heavy bespoke development later

Questions

Common questions

Is Wix actually good for SEO?

Wix Studio is far better for SEO than its old reputation suggests. You control titles, metadata, structure, and schema. It will not out-muscle a purpose-built custom site in a cut-throat niche, but for most businesses a properly set up Wix site ranks perfectly well.

Can a Wix site not look like a template?

Yes. The template look comes from using templates as-is. I design Wix Studio layouts from scratch around your brand, so the result looks bespoke.

What if I outgrow Wix later?

That is a fair concern, so I build with it in mind. Clean content structure and a sensible URL setup make a future migration to WordPress or a custom build much less painful.
