Straight talk

Is this the right call for you?

I would rather point you at the right tool than sell you the wrong one. Here is when this fits, and when it does not.

Great fit when…

  • You are a DTC or ecommerce brand that lives and dies by conversion
  • You have outgrown a template and need real customization
  • You need Shopify Plus for custom checkout, Scripts and Functions, or B2B
  • You are migrating from Magento, WooCommerce, or a legacy platform

Probably not, if…

  • You have an enormously complex B2B catalog (Magento may fit better)
  • You only need a tiny store and basic Shopify is genuinely enough
  • You want a content-first site more than a store (WordPress may win)

Questions

Common questions

Shopify or Shopify Plus, which do I need?

Standard Shopify is plenty for most stores. You move to Plus when you need custom checkout logic, scripts and functions, B2B wholesale, higher API limits, or multi-store, usually at meaningful revenue. I will tell you honestly which tier your situation calls for.

Can you migrate us to Shopify?

Yes. Migrations from Magento, WooCommerce, and legacy platforms are common. The priority is protecting SEO: mapping redirects, preserving URL structure where possible, and moving products and content cleanly.

Can you customize the Shopify checkout?

On Shopify Plus, yes, through checkout extensibility, Scripts, and Functions. On standard Shopify the checkout is more locked down, which is itself a reason some brands upgrade to Plus.
