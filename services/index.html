Common questions

Before you pick a pillar.

Can I hire you for just a website, or just SEO?

Yes. Plenty of clients start with a single build or an SEO engagement. The advantage of one partner is that the moment you want the other, there's no re-briefing a new vendor — I already know your site inside out.

Which website platform should I use?

It depends on who runs the site day to day and what it has to do. WordPress for content-heavy marketing sites, Shopify or Shopify Plus for serious commerce, Magento for complex catalogs, Wix for lean teams, and custom code or headless when you need something the templates can't do. I recommend the platform that fits your team, not the one I feel like building.

Do you work with our existing developers or marketing team?

Often, yes. I can lead the whole engagement or plug in as the senior specialist alongside your in-house team, hand off clean documentation, and keep everyone pointed at the same pipeline goals.
