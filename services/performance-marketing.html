Questions

Common questions

How is this different from hiring a media-buying agency?

Most agencies optimize the channel they bill on. Because I also build the site and run the SEO, I optimize the whole path from click to customer — and I will happily move budget away from a channel that is not earning it.

Which ad platforms do you work with?

Google Ads (search, Performance Max, YouTube), Meta (Facebook and Instagram), and LinkedIn for B2B. The mix depends on where your buyers actually are — not on a package.

How do you report results?

Against pipeline: qualified leads, opportunities, and revenue influence, with GA4 and your CRM as the source of truth. If a number is modeled or assumed, the report says so plainly.
